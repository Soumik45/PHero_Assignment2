BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is important as it provides a simple, quick way to assess whether an individual's weight is in a healthy range, helping to identify potential health risks like obesity, heart disease, and diabetes. It's a widely used tool to guide healthcare decisions and promote better lifestyle choices.
